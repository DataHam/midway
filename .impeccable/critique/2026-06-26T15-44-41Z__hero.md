## Findings (ranked)

### 1. [Refine ‚Äî highest lever] The scrim mutes the photo's warmth

The 0.62 top band lays a murky navy wash over the light studio backdrop where the *empty headroom* sits, and the blue cast desaturates the couple. The faces land in the lightest zone (good), but the overall hero reads cool/muted against a brief whose north star is **warmth**.
- **Move:** lighten the top band (‚âà0.62‚Üí0.30‚Äì0.40) so the upper area isn't a muddy wash, and/or replace the linear top with a **radial/elliptical** scrim that keeps the faces luminous while still darkening the lower third for text. ### 2. [Verify] Hero vs social use different filenames of the same shoot

### 3. [Minor ‚Äî a11y] Teal subtext at 16px
"Tap whoever you're here to see." renders `#7DC2B6` (coastal-teal) at 16px. Over the **dark** lower scrim it clears ~6:1 (fine). ### 4. [Optional] Hero H1 is understated
