# Critique — danny.html & helen.html (the two person pages)

Date: 2026-06-26 · Verified in headless Chromium at 1280 & 390, with a computed-contrast pass. Same lens as the index/hero critiques.

## Verdict: B− — functional, on-brand, and styled, but held back by one real a11y fail, desktop dead-space, content redundancy, and several Danny↔Helen inconsistencies.

Both pages share a layout: sticky navy nav → white profile card (portrait left / details right) → a 3-card section → footer. Mobile (single column) is clean on both. The problems concentrate on **desktop** and in **parity between the two pages**.

## Findings (ranked)

### 1. [P1 · WCAG AA] Teal role line on white = 2.05:1
"BSc, MBA — Principal Consultant" / "PhD — Research Consultant" render `#7DC2B6` (coastal-teal) at 16px/600 on white — **2.05:1**, far below the 4.5:1 floor. This is precisely DESIGN.md's **"Yellow-On-Navy Rule"** (teal/yellow forbidden as small text on white). On the index it's fine because the teal labels sit on the *navy* zone (~6:1); on these white cards it fails.
- **Fix:** introduce an on-white teal (darken to ~`#3E7E72`/`#356B61` for ≥4.5:1) used only for text-on-light, or set the role line in navy/slate. `/impeccable colorize` or `clarify`.

### 2. [P2 · Layout] Desktop profile card is half-empty
The card is `max-w-7xl` but the details column fills only the left ~55%; the right ~40% is blank white. Reads unbalanced and unfinished on desktop (mobile is fine — single column).
- **Fix:** cap the card width (e.g. `max-w-3xl/4xl` centered), or use the right space deliberately (wider measure, a second column, or a portrait that spans more). `/impeccable layout`.

### 3. [P2 · Content] The middle list duplicates the section below
Danny: the card's "Consulting Services" bullet list (IT Strategy / Digital Transformation / Healthcare IT) repeats verbatim as the 3 cards in the "Consulting Services" section. Helen: the card's "Professional Profile" trio (Clinical Research / Publications / Data Analysis) repeats as "Selected Research" cards. Same content twice on one short page.
- **Fix:** drop the in-card list (let the section own it), or make the card a one-line summary and the section the detail. `/impeccable distill`.

### 4. [P2 · Anti-pattern] Identical 3-card icon grid
The lower section on both pages is the textbook icon + heading + text ×3 equal-card grid (an impeccable ban). It's a small set so it's borderline, but it reads templated.
- **Fix:** differentiate the cards (vary size/emphasis), or switch to a typographic list / definition layout. `/impeccable layout` or `bolder`.

### 5. [P2 · Parity] Portrait backgrounds differ and don't match the index
Helen's profile photo is on **dark green**; Danny's on **cream** — clashing with each other and with the unified **`#F1F3F4`** discs on the index diptych. The identity-parity fix never reached the person-page photos.
- **Fix:** regenerate `HelenSquareProfile`/`DannySquareProfile` on `#F1F3F4` (same segmentation pipeline used for the index portraits). `/impeccable polish`.

### 6. [P2 · Parity/IA] Nav differs between the two pages
Danny's nav: `Danny · Helen · Contact Danny · Contact Helen` (links to both people, no Home). Helen's nav: `Home · Helen · Contact Danny · Contact Helen` — **no link to Danny's page** (only his contact), plus a Home item Danny's lacks. From Helen's page you cannot reach Danny's profile.
- **Fix:** one shared nav on both (recommend `Danny · Helen · Contact Danny · Contact Helen`, current page marked active). `/impeccable clarify`/`harden`.

### 7. [P3 · Parity] Card middle-slot uses different components
Same slot, two patterns: Danny = bullet list; Helen = 3 icon blocks (which also makes Helen's card much taller than Danny's). Pick one pattern for both.

## Positive findings
- Both fully styled (compiled CSS, Montserrat), 0 console errors.
- **Save contact** is a single confident gated CTA (Turnstile vCard); phone no longer inline.
- Mobile is clean and well-stacked on both.
- Palette (navy/teal/yellow/blue) is coherent; nav, footer, focus states present; headings exist.
- Contact links (`#385C8F` blue on white ≈ 5.9:1) pass.

## Recommended order
1. **[P1] colorize/clarify** — fix the teal-on-white role line (2.05:1) on both pages.
2. **[P2] clarify/harden** — unify the nav across both pages.
3. **[P2] polish** — regenerate both profile photos on `#F1F3F4` for parity with the index.
4. **[P2] layout** — cap the card width / fill the desktop dead-space; unify the card middle slot.
5. **[P2] distill** — remove the in-card list duplicated by the section below.
6. **[P2] layout/bolder** — rework the identical 3-card grid.

## Resolution (applied — user chose "Everything")
Verified in headless Chromium at 1280 & 390 on both pages.
- **[P1] Contrast:** added a `--color-teal-ink: #2D6A5E` token; role lines now `text-teal-ink` = **6.3:1** on white (was 2.05:1). WCAG AA pass.
- **[P2] Nav parity:** Helen's `Home` → `Danny`; both pages now share `Danny · Helen · Contact Danny · Contact Helen` (current page active). You can reach Danny's profile from Helen's page.
- **[P2] Portrait parity:** both profile photos now point at the existing `#F1F3F4` composites (`DannyPortrait`/`HelenPortrait`) — same ground as the index diptych. No regeneration needed (reused the segmented assets). Green/cream mismatch gone.
- **[P2] Card dead-space:** capped the card to `max-w-4xl`, image `md:w-2/5` + content `md:w-3/5` as a flex column with the CTA `mt-auto` — the empty desktop right-half is gone.
- **[P2] De-dup:** removed the in-card "Consulting Services" list (Danny) and "Professional Profile" trio (Helen); that content lives only in the section below now.
- **[P2] Anti-pattern:** the lower sections are no longer white card grids — de-carded to clean typographic columns (colored icon chip + heading + text) on a `bg-mist` ground.
- **[P3] Middle-slot parity:** both card bodies now share one structure (identity + contact + CTA).

Net: person pages **B− → A−**, and consistent with each other and the index. Both fully verified; 0 console errors.
