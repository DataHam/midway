# Product

## Register
brand

## Users
Calgary Stampede attendees, primarily on mobile devices, walking the festival grounds and actively looking for specific food booths or browsing options.

## Product Purpose
An interactive, mobile-optimized map to discover, locate, and learn about midway food vendors at the 2026 Calgary Stampede. Success means users can quickly find a booth without getting frustrated by the UI.

## Brand Personality
Festive, energetic, premium but accessible. Rooted in the Calgary Stampede identity (Stampede Red and Gold) with a modern, dark-mode aesthetic.

## Anti-references
- Generic corporate dashboards
- Dense, text-heavy directory lists that hide the map
- Unresponsive layouts that require zooming on mobile

## Design Principles
1. **Mobile-First Utility**: The UI must be usable with one hand while walking outdoors.
2. **The Map is the Hero**: Interface elements (sidebar, popups) should float above and complement the map, not crowd it out.
3. **Outdoor Legibility**: High contrast is essential for reading under direct sunlight.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance. High contrast text, clear touch targets (minimum 44x44px) for mobile users.
